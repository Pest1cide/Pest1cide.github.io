02 Research

Full research page
Master’s thesis · 2026 Idiap / EPFL · Robot Learning & Interaction

Self-supervised configuration-space distance functions

SCDF is a self-supervised, physics-informed neural distance function that removes the expensive configuration-space ground-truth labelling required by CDF, while preserving its one-step inverse-kinematics property.

Integrated into learning-based and gradient-based pipelines for controlled comparisons against JSDF, CDF, RDF, and PointNet baselines — from 2-D reinforcement-learning tasks to 7-DoF manipulators.

Read more

With Sylvain Calinon, Dominik Baumann and Yiming Li

LASA · EPFL · 2025 — Reactive motion

Optimization-based vs. reactive dynamical systems

A comprehensive benchmark of GPU-accelerated MPPI control (NVIDIA cuRobo) against geometrically-guided hybrid dynamical systems for reactive motion planning.

Built a hybrid motion-generation framework for a 7-DoF Franka Emika Panda that couples diffeomorphic transformations with implicit representations (Neural-JSDF / CDF) to guarantee stable obstacle avoidance.

Read more

With Aude Billard and Rui Wu

Aalto · 2024 — 25 Multi-agent planning

Scalability of multi-agent path finding

Introduced and optimized hybrid velocity-obstacle methods for decentralized collision avoidance. In simulations with 12 agents, the approach improved overall planning efficiency by 10% over global planners.

With Tomasz Kucner, Mobile Robotics Group
