Life beyond the lab

Places change the way I see problems.

I grew into robotics across Shenzhen, Helsinki, and Lausanne. Each place brought a different research culture, landscape, and way of thinking. This page is a small visual record of the world around the work.

A few constants

What stays with me between projects.

01

Learning by building

I understand an idea best when I can turn it into a model, run it, break it, and make the next experiment sharper.

02

Crossing disciplines

Robotics keeps me moving between mechanics, control, software, learning, and the human context around a system.

03

Looking outward

Travel and photography create space to notice structure, scale, and motion outside a simulator or laboratory.

Background
